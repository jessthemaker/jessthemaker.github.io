Local Liquid Beer Series üçª

Chad Zimar, master brewer and owner of Steuben Brewing Company, approached me about a new series of beers that would focus on using local NY state ingredients; Local Liquid.

Chad knew he wanted the design to be bold and modern. When I heard "local liquid" I immediately thought of all of the beautiful rivers and lakes NY state is known for. I began digging in the public domain archives for satellite images of NY waterways.

I used the images for background texture and paired them with a brightly-colored linear gradient.
